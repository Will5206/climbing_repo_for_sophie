CLUB INFO

General Information

FAQ #1: Can I climb even if I am completely new to the sport?

Yes! The Climbing Club is open to everyone, whether climbing with us is your first time on the wall, or you’ve sent every v17!

FAQ #2: Is there a group chat?

Yes! Our main form of contact is our GroupMe.

FAQ # 3: Does the club have social media I can follow?

Yes! Check out our Instagram page: @tulaneclimbingclub

We are still working on a system to get first-time climbers a free pass. For the moment, message anyone from the e-board saying it's your first time and we will try to hook you up with a guest pass.

New Orleans Boulder Lounge (NOBL) General info

We climb at the New Orleans Boulder Lounge! It is roughly 5 miles way from campus.

• NOBL website: climbnobl.com


Online Signup and Waiver
If you have never been to NOBL you can fill out the online waiver and sign up before you arrive to save time. Link to waiver

Membership Info
NOBL offers a tiered pricing system in which you can choose how much you would like to pay!

• Reduced rate: $60
• Standard rate: $75
• Sustainer rate: $90
• Day Pass: $16 / $20 / $24 (depending on how much you want to pay)
• Tulane Club Student Membership: $25 start up fee + full month of dues upfront | $45/mo

Requirements for the Tulane Club Student Membership:
• Must have paid club dues, documentation required from climbing club to register (we provide NOBL this information)
• Dates of use: September 1 - May 31 (Will be canceled on May 31 for summer months)

Entry Hours for the Tulane Club Student Membership:
Tuesday-Friday | 6:30am-5:00pm* (the system will only allow scan-ins until 4:00pm, and we ask that folks head out at 5:00pm)
Monday-Friday | 7:30pm-10:00pm
Saturday & Sunday | 12:00pm-7:00pm

Club Dues

Dues are $30 and must be paid by September 25th

By paying club dues, you are given access to:
• Priority to van transportation
• Tulane Club Student NOBL membership
• Priority for trips and events
